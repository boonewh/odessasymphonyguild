Odessa Symphony Guild

Enriching West Texas Through Music Since 1958

For over six decades, we have supported the Midland-Odessa Symphony & Chorale, bringing world-class music, culture, and educational opportunities to our community through dedicated volunteer service and financial support.

Join Our Mission

Our Heritage

A legacy of dedication to the arts and our community

The Odessa Symphony Guild was founded in 1958 by a visionary group of women determined to bring fine arts and culture to West Texas. What began as a passionate effort to support local musicians has grown into a thriving non-profit organization that continues to honor the spirit of its founding members.

Through more than sixty-five years of service, the Guild has raised thousands of dollars and contributed countless volunteer hours to support the Midland-Odessa Symphony & Chorale. This partnership enables the symphony to provide our community with exceptional educational programs and world-class concerts that enrich the cultural fabric of West Texas.

Our commitment extends beyond financial support. We actively engage in promoting concerts, selling memberships, ushering at performances, hosting receptions, serving musicians, and ensuring every concert is a memorable experience for our community.

Our Mission

To provide unwavering support to the Midland-Odessa Symphony & Chorale through volunteer service and financial contributions, ensuring that exceptional music education and performances remain accessible to current and future generations of West Texans.

We believe in the transformative power of music and its ability to unite and inspire our community.

How We Serve

Supporting the symphony through dedicated volunteer programs

Active Members

Our active members form the backbone of the Guild, volunteering their time to promote concerts, usher performances, host receptions, and serve musicians. They ensure every symphony event runs smoothly and every guest feels welcomed.

Patron Support

Patrons provide vital financial support to the Guild and the Midland-Odessa Symphony & Chorale. Their generous contributions help fund educational programs, concert productions, and operational needs that make our mission possible.

Symphony SoundBites

Our volunteers work tirelessly to prepare and execute Symphony SoundBites events, creating intimate musical experiences that bring our community closer to the artistry of the symphony and its talented musicians.

Belles & Beaux Program

Developing tomorrow's leaders through service and appreciation of the arts

Cultivating Future Leaders

The Odessa Symphony Guild proudly sponsors the Belles and Beaux program for young men and women in grades 9-12. This distinguished program introduces students to the arts while instilling the values of community service and leadership.

  • Four years of meaningful service to the community
  • Hands-on experience supporting symphony events and concerts
  • Development of leadership skills and social grace
  • Cultivation of lifelong appreciation for the performing arts
  • Recognition and celebration at the annual Symphony Gala Ball

Annual Symphony Gala Ball

An elegant evening celebrating service, achievement, and the arts

A Night to Remember

Our annual Symphony Gala Ball is the highlight of the social season, honoring our dedicated Belles and Beaux while raising vital funds to support the Midland-Odessa Symphony & Chorale.

The evening features the formal presentation of our freshman, sophomore, and junior Belles and Beaux, culminating in a special recognition of our seniors and their four years of exemplary service to the Guild and our community.

This elegant affair brings together Guild members, patrons, families, and symphony supporters for an unforgettable celebration of music, service, and community.
